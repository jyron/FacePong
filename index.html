Your face is the paddle. Best-of-5 pong with selfies, neon, and bragging rights.

FAQ

How do I play?
Tap a face slot on the home screen to snap a selfie or pick a photo — your face becomes your paddle. Then play the computer, hit Quick Match, or create a game and share the code with a friend. First to 5 points wins.
Where do my photos go?
Your face photo is processed on your device and stored only on your device. In an online match it is sent once, over an encrypted connection, to your opponent’s screen for the duration of that match — it is never saved on our servers. Details in the privacy policy.
Do I need an account?
No. There are no accounts, no sign-ups, and no profiles.
My friend can’t join with my code.
Codes are live only while the host is on the “share this code” screen. Make sure both phones are online, and that the code is typed exactly (4 characters, no spaces).
